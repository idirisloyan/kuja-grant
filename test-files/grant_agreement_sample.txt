GRANT AGREEMENT

BETWEEN
Global Health Foundation ("the Donor")
AND
[Grantee Organization Name] ("the Grantee")

GRANT REFERENCE: GHF-2026-XXXX
EFFECTIVE DATE: [Start Date]
END DATE: [End Date]
TOTAL GRANT AMOUNT: $500,000 USD

ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE
This grant is provided for the implementation of the project titled "Community Health Workers Scale-Up Program" as described in the approved proposal dated [date] and budget attached as Annex A.

ARTICLE 2: GRANT PERIOD
The grant period shall be twenty-four (24) months from the effective date. All funds must be expended within the grant period unless a no-cost extension is approved in writing.

ARTICLE 3: DISBURSEMENT SCHEDULE
Disbursements shall be made as follows:
- First tranche (40%): $200,000 upon signing
- Second tranche (30%): $150,000 upon acceptance of first semi-annual report
- Third tranche (20%): $100,000 upon acceptance of second semi-annual report
- Final tranche (10%): $50,000 upon acceptance of third semi-annual report

ARTICLE 4: REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Financial Reports
The Grantee shall submit quarterly financial reports within thirty (30) days of the end of each quarter. Financial reports shall include:
a) Statement of expenditure by budget line item
b) Budget vs. actual comparison with variance analysis
c) Bank reconciliation statement
d) Cash flow projection for next quarter
e) List of procurements exceeding $5,000

4.2 Narrative/Progress Reports
The Grantee shall submit semi-annual narrative reports within forty-five (45) days of the end of each six-month period. Narrative reports shall include:
a) Executive summary of progress
b) Activities completed against work plan
c) Progress against indicators and targets
d) Beneficiary data disaggregated by gender and age
e) Challenges encountered and mitigation measures
f) Lessons learned and best practices
g) Updated work plan for next period
h) Success stories and case studies (minimum 2)

4.3 Impact Report
The Grantee shall submit an annual impact report within sixty (60) days of each anniversary of the grant start date. The impact report shall include:
a) Outcome-level results against the logical framework
b) Contribution to Sustainable Development Goals
c) Community feedback and satisfaction data
d) Environmental and social impact assessment
e) Value for money analysis

4.4 Final Report
Within ninety (90) days of the end of the grant period, the Grantee shall submit a final report including:
a) Comprehensive narrative covering the entire grant period
b) Final financial report with audited expenditure statement
c) Asset inventory and disposition plan
d) Sustainability assessment and transition plan
e) Final beneficiary numbers and outcome data
f) Recommendations for future programming

4.5 Audit Requirements
The Grantee shall:
a) Submit an annual audit report within six (6) months of the fiscal year end
b) Ensure the audit is conducted by an independent, qualified audit firm
c) Include a management letter with the audit report
d) Address audit findings within three (3) months

ARTICLE 5: KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS
The following indicators shall be tracked and reported:
1. Number of community health workers trained and active (Target: 50)
2. Number of households visited per quarter (Target: 5,000)
3. Facility referrals per quarter (Target: 800)
4. Antenatal care enrollment (4+ visits) (Target: 45% coverage)
5. Skilled birth attendance rate (Target: 55%)
6. Under-5 vaccination coverage (Target: 80%)
7. Community satisfaction score (Target: >80%)
8. Budget utilization rate (Target: >85%)
9. Staff retention rate (Target: >90%)
10. Number of health education sessions (Target: 200/year)

ARTICLE 6: COMPLIANCE
6.1 The Grantee shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
6.2 The Grantee shall maintain a policy against terrorism financing and money laundering.
6.3 The Grantee shall screen all sub-grantees and vendors against international sanctions lists.
6.4 The Grantee shall maintain zero tolerance for fraud, corruption, and sexual exploitation.

ARTICLE 7: MODIFICATIONS
Any modification to this agreement must be requested in writing at least thirty (30) days before the proposed change and requires written approval from the Donor.

ARTICLE 8: TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with sixty (60) days written notice. In case of termination, the Grantee shall return any unexpended funds within thirty (30) days.
