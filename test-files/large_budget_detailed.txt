BUDGET NOTES:

1. All costs are in United States Dollars (USD). Local currency payments will be converted at the UN operational exchange rate prevailing at the time of transaction.

2. Personnel costs include basic salary only. Social security contributions and statutory benefits are included as part of the salary costs.

3. Equipment purchased with grant funds remains the property of the donor until the end of the project, at which point a disposition plan will be agreed upon.

4. The indirect cost rate of 7% is applied to total direct costs and covers headquarters management support, shared institutional services, and governance costs.

5. Reallocations between budget lines within a category of up to 10% do not require donor approval. Reallocations between categories or exceeding 10% require prior written approval.

6. The contingency reserve of 2% may only be utilized with prior written donor approval and will be subject to the same reporting and audit requirements as all other budget lines.

7. All procurement will follow HADI's procurement policy, which requires:
   - Purchases under $500: Direct purchase with receipt
   - $500 - $5,000: Three written quotations
   - $5,000 - $25,000: Sealed bid process
   - Over $25,000: Formal tender process

8. VAT/tax exemptions will be sought where applicable. Where exemptions are not obtainable, taxes are included in the unit costs above.
