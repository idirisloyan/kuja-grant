Objects and Purposes:
The Organization is registered for the following purposes:
1. To promote community health and wellbeing through preventive and curative health programs
2. To strengthen local capacity for sustainable development
3. To empower women and youth through education and skills training
4. To promote good governance and civic participation
5. To respond to humanitarian emergencies in affected communities

Conditions of Registration:
1. The Organization shall comply with all provisions of the NGO Co-ordination Act
2. Annual returns and audited accounts shall be filed within 6 months of the financial year end
3. Any amendment to the constitution shall be notified to the Board within 30 days
4. The Organization shall not engage in activities inconsistent with its registered objects
